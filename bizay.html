Bizay

Bizay started in 2013, in Lisbon, Portugal. They are a company that seeks differentiation through excellence and customer satisfaction.
Their mission is helping and inspiring Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs) to have successful communication, changing the way they develop and implement their marketing strategy.

Value Proposition

  • Instantly present the price of all products and, when any feature of the articles is changed, the recalculate the value immediately. Thus, it is possible to establish all comparisons in real time and at no cost.

  • Ensuring the lowest prices on the market, matching any outside quotes with a lower value.

  • Working so as not to defraud the expectations of the clients with a policy that if the products do not match up to what was promised, they reprint or return the money.

  • Having a dedicated technical support department to answer questions and requests for information.

  • Combine professional quality designs with an economical price. To that effect, have developed a practical and intuitive process so that customers can customize their products quickly.

  • Promote efficiency and respect the deadlines set by customers.
